OUR MENU

Espresso (ess-PRESS-oh) is a full-flavored, concentrated form of coffee that is served in “shots.”

AMERICANO is coffee consisting of espresso diluted with hot water : café americano

A cappuccino is an espresso drink with steamed milk, milk foam and espresso

A frappé (pronounced frap-pay) is an iced beverage that has been shaken, blended or beaten to produce a tasty, foamy, and refreshing drink.

A Mocha Coffee a shot of espresso is combined with a chocolate powder or syrup, followed by milk or cream

A Hot Choco is a heated drink consisting of shaved chocolate, melted chocolate or cocoa powder, heated milk or water, and usually a sweetener like whipped cream or marshmallows.

A Latte is a coffee beverage of Italian origin made with espresso and steamed milk.

A Belgian Waffle square-shaped breakfast cake with deeply indented or pressed pockets evenly distributed around the surface of the Waffle

A chocolate pudding is a decadent dessert made out with Hershey's chocolate

A Cheesecake is a sweet dessert consisting of one or more layers

A Chocolate Doughnut are oblong yeast donuts with a chocolate glaze

A croissant is a laminated, yeast-leavened bakery product that contains dough/roll-in fat layers to create a flaky, crispy texture
